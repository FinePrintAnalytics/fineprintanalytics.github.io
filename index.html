About

Fine Print Analytics is a fully automated sports analytics platform running on Google Cloud Platform. Data is scraped, modeled, and published four times daily without manual intervention.

The NBA pipeline covers every game on the slate - team form, pace, rest, travel, injury adjustments, and odds movement - distilled into previews, picks, and a live data API. The same architecture is being extended to MLB for the 2026 season.
